# ADR-019: SymmetryDispatchContext Lock-Free Refactor

## Status
Proposed (Build 1111.003-v28.0-adr019)

## Context
P7 Sentinel found 12 structural violations in the V12.002 substrate:
- 11 residual `lock(ctx.Sync)` sites in Symmetry and Orders modules.
- 1 banned `dailySummaryLock` field declaration.

These violate the **Sovereign Substrate Mandate** (Zero-Lock Policy). Previous refactor attempts were BLOCKED due to R1-R4 safety risks (ghost orders, torn reads).

## Decision
We will refactor `SymmetryDispatchContext` to a 100% lock-free Actor model:
1. **Atomic Fields**: Use `long`-backed bit-conversion for `MasterAnchorPrice` and `MasterWeightedFill` to prevent torn reads on x86.
2. **Concurrent State**: Replace `HashSet<string>` with `ConcurrentDictionary<string, byte>` for `FollowerEntries`.
3. **Volatile Flags**: Use `volatile` for non-participatory state flags.
4. **Safety Rollback**: Implement broker-side cancellation *before* local state purge (Fixes R1).

## Consequences
- Zero thread-blocking on the Symmetry hot-path.
- 100% compliance with Audit Gate 2.
- Increased complexity in state synchronization (requires CAS loops).
