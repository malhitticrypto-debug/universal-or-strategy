Sovereign Modularity: Why this matters

1. Platform Independence: The logic we are building (250ns bitwise dispatch) lives in the "Sovereign Core." It does not know NinjaTrader exists.

2. Total Replacement: To replace NinjaTrader, we only swap the **L1 Physical Bridge**. The 243ns performance we found today carries over to the new platform 1:1.

3. No Intermediaries: In the final Nexus OS, the "order" flows from your click to the exchange in a single direct pipe, with zero Bloatware in between.
